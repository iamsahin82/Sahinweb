WhatsApp Marketing in India: The Channel Your Customers Already Live On

By Sahin Molla · 2026-06-05 · 5 min read

Ask yourself: when did you last ignore a WhatsApp message for more than an hour? Exactly. Now compare that with the marketing emails sitting unread in your promotions tab. For Indian businesses, WhatsApp is not "another channel" — it is the channel, and most companies still barely use it.

Why WhatsApp outperforms email in India

Open rates tell the story: industry studies consistently place WhatsApp message open rates above 90%, with most messages read within minutes. Email campaigns in India typically see 20–30% opens on a good day. Your customers have not stopped reading — they have just moved rooms.

What businesses actually use it for

The serious use cases go far beyond broadcasts. Automated order confirmations build instant trust for e-commerce. Abandoned-cart nudges recover sales that were one distraction away. Appointment reminders cut no-shows for clinics and salons. Catalogue messages let customers browse without leaving the chat. We integrated the WhatsApp Business API for a live store handling hundreds of orders — confirmations now happen automatically, in seconds, with zero manual work.

Doing it right (and legally)

WhatsApp marketing must be permission-based: customers opt in, templates get approved, and frequency stays respectful. Spam gets numbers blocked fast — strategy and restraint matter as much as technology.

Start simple

You do not need a complex bot on day one. A verified business profile, instant order or enquiry confirmations, and one thoughtful follow-up flow will outperform most email programmes immediately.
