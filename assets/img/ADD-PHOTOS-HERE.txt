PHOTOS & VIDEO — Shahin Web
============================
Already working:
  sahin.jpg      -> Your photo (Team, About, Book-Call pages)
  mwk.jpg        -> Maulana Wahiduddin Khan (Portfolio card)
  goodword.jpg   -> Goodword Books logo (Clients strip)

To add team member photos:
  Save as assets/img/team/firstname.jpg (lowercase first name)
  Examples: team/ashif.jpg, team/afruja.jpg, team/azizul.jpg
  No photo? A styled initials avatar shows automatically.

To enable the video popup (shows once, 5 seconds after page load):
  Save your video as  Ad-Duha.mp4  in the main site folder
  (next to index.html). If the file is missing, no popup appears.
  You can switch it off anytime in Admin > Video Popup.
