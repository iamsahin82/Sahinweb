Our Story

Built in Bengal. Building for All of India.

Shahin Web grew out of Diamond Harbour with a simple belief: businesses should only pay for marketing that delivers measurable outcomes. No fluff. No filler. Just results.

The proof came quickly: we took a heritage publisher's quiet online presence to 186K monthly sessions at a sustained 4.1x ROAS — building the entire stack ourselves: server, REST API, Supabase database, storefront, and WhatsApp automation. Today we build for clients across Kolkata, Delhi, and Pan-India — including Medha Care, QuestX, and Sky Blue Publication.

We are not the biggest agency in India, and we're not trying to be. We are building the most trusted one.
